Lauren Liebe

Digital CV and Teaching Resources

Lauren Liebe is an assistant teaching professor of game studies at Penn State Erie, The Behrend College. Previously, she was a postdoctoral researcher at the Center of Digital Humanities Research (CoDHR) at Texas A&M University. She is the Director of the Virtual and Augmented Reality and Interactive Arts (VARIA) Lab, as well as the editor and creator of Digital Restoration Drama, soon to be hosted through Linked Early Modern Drama Online.
